Take Away Noodles

Delicious noodles made with minimal effort on your part

Ingredients

  • Phone
  • The will power to overcome social anxiety and talk to someone on the phone
  • Patience
  • Maybe a book or something to kill the time?

Steps

  1. Call your takeaway restaurant of choice
  2. Ask for noodles
  3. Wait. Maybe read that book while you wait
  4. Answer the door and pay the deliery driver. (Don't forget to tip)
  5. Enjoy!
