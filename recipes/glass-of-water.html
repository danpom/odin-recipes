Glass Of Water

Scrumpcious glass of water to quench anyone's thirst

Ingredients

  • Bottle of water (preferably chilled)
  • Recepticle for the liquid. We like to use a glass tumbler

Steps

  1. Place glass on a stable surface
  2. Open bottle cap
  3. Take bottle in hand and pour the water in to the emtpy glass
  4. Congratulations! You were a glass is empty kind of person and now your glass is full!
  5. Enjoy!
